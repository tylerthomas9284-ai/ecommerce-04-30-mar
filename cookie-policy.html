Cookie Policy

What cookies we use, why we use them, and how to control them.

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how MANE. ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website mane.site. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or actions over time.

Cookies are not viruses or malware — they are standard technology used by virtually all websites to improve functionality and user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential / Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function. They cannot be switched off. They include:

  • mane_cart — stores your shopping cart contents in localStorage (session-based)
  • mane_cookies — remembers your cookie consent choice
  • Session cookies — maintain your browsing session on our site

Performance / Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve it. We use Google Analytics for this purpose. Data collected is anonymized and aggregated — we cannot identify individual users from this data.

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Traffic sources (how you arrived at our site)
  • Device type, browser, and screen resolution
  • Bounce rate and conversion tracking

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced features and personalization, such as remembering your country or currency preference, your recently viewed products, and whether you have dismissed certain prompts.

Marketing / Targeting Cookies

These cookies are set by advertising partners and help deliver relevant ads to you across the web. They track your browsing across sites to build a profile of your interests. You can opt out of these at any time (see Section 5). Marketing cookies we may use include:

  • Meta Pixel (Facebook / Instagram advertising)
  • TikTok Pixel
  • Google Ads remarketing cookies
  • Pinterest Tag

3. First-Party vs Third-Party Cookies

First-party cookies are set directly by MANE. for the purposes described above.

Third-party cookies are set by our partners (analytics, advertising, payment processors). These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies — we encourage you to review them:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Meta Privacy Policy
  • PayPal Privacy Policy

4. Cookie Duration

Cookies can be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (remain on your device for a set period). Most of our functional and analytics cookies are persistent with a lifespan of 30 days to 2 years. You can delete them at any time via your browser settings.

5. How to Control Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

  • Cookie banner: When you first visit our site, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies via our cookie banner
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. See your browser's help section:
    • Google Chrome
    • Mozilla Firefox
    • Apple Safari
    • Microsoft Edge
  • Opt-out tools: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Do Not Track: If your browser sends a "Do Not Track" signal, we will endeavor to honor it for non-essential tracking

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website, including your shopping cart and checkout experience.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices change or as required by law. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
