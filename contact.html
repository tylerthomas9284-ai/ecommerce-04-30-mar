Common Questions

FAQ

How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping takes 3–7 business days. Expedited (2-day) and overnight options are available at checkout. All orders are dispatched within 1 business day.
Can I return or exchange? +
Yes! We offer 30-day hassle-free returns and exchanges. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Email us at hello@mane.site to start a return.
Is Cash on Delivery available? +
Yes — COD is available for all US delivery addresses. Select "Cash on Delivery" at checkout. No extra fee. You pay the driver when your order arrives.
Do you ship internationally? +
We currently ship to the US, Canada, UK, Australia, and most of Europe. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 business days. COD is only available in the US.
Are your products safe for all hair types? +
Absolutely. All MANE. products are designed for all hair types — fine, thick, curly, wavy, natural, and color-treated. Our satin and silk products are especially recommended for color-treated and fragile hair.
