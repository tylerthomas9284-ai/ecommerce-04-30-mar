Our Mission

Accessories That
Work As Hard
As You Do.

We started MANE. in 2022 because we were tired of buying accessories that broke after a week, slipped out mid-day, or left painful dents in our hair. There had to be something better.

So we sourced the same premium materials used in luxury salons — genuine satin, mulberry silk, reinforced velvet — and made them accessible without the absurd markup.

Every MANE. product is tested by our team before it ships. If it doesn't hold up in real life, it doesn't make the cut. Simple as that.
